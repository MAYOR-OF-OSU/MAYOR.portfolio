MAYOR'S BIOGRAPHY

Legacy of Love and Learning: The Story of Mayokun Samuel Olowofola-deola

Born on January 18, 2001, Mayokun Samuel Olowofola-deola emerged from a close-knit, middle-class Nigerian family rooted in Ifon, Ose Local Government Area of Ondo State. As one of four siblings in a loving household of six, Mayokun's early life was shaped by the guidance of his father, the late Mr. Olowofola Julius Adeola, and his mother,Mrs. Olowofola Funke Helen, alongside his two sisters and younger brother.
